Construction work in the Netherlands - Drywall Installer

This vacancy is for drywall and suspended ceiling installation work on interior construction and finishing projects.

Hourly pay

19,20 € gross / hour

Responsibilities

  • Install profiles, boards and drywall construction elements
  • Work on suspended ceilings and partition walls
  • Cut, fit and prepare materials for installation
  • Follow technical instructions and supervisor guidance

Requirements

  • Experience in finishing or drywall work is welcome
  • Ability to use basic power tools safely
  • Communicative English or Dutch language skills
  • Accuracy and attention to finished work quality

We offer

  • Dutch employment contract
  • Accommodation arranged by the employer
  • Health insurance
  • Support from Polish-speaking consultants
