Construction work in the Netherlands - Steel Structure Installer

This role is for experienced workers who can support steel frame assembly, beam positioning and installation work on large construction sites.

Hourly pay

22,54 € gross / hour

Responsibilities

  • Install and position steel structure components
  • Support lifting, guiding and alignment of beams and frames
  • Prepare work areas and assist with basic measurements
  • Follow supervisor instructions, site procedures and safety rules

Requirements

  • Experience with steel structures is preferred
  • Ability to work safely at height
  • Communicative English or Dutch language skills
  • Team-oriented attitude and attention to detail

We offer

  • Dutch employment contract
  • Accommodation arranged by the employer
  • Health insurance
  • Support from Polish-speaking consultants
