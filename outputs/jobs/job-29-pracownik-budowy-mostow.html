Construction work in the Netherlands - Bridge Construction Worker

We are recruiting construction workers for bridge and road infrastructure projects involving concrete placement, reinforcement support and site preparation.

Hourly pay

21,35 € gross / hour

Responsibilities

  • Prepare surfaces and work areas before concrete pouring
  • Assist with reinforcement, concrete placement and finishing work
  • Support cleaning, setup and material movement on bridge sites
  • Follow safety procedures around heavy equipment and active work zones

Requirements

  • Experience in road, bridge or concrete construction is welcome
  • Willingness to work outdoors in changing weather conditions
  • Communicative English or Dutch language skills
  • Responsible attitude and good work organization

We offer

  • Dutch employment contract
  • Accommodation arranged by the employer
  • Health insurance
  • Support from Polish-speaking consultants
