Construction work in the Netherlands - Paver

This position involves paving and finishing work for terraces, driveways, walkways and stone slab surfaces.

Hourly pay

19,85 € gross / hour

Responsibilities

  • Lay paving slabs, stones and surface elements
  • Prepare sub-bases and level working surfaces
  • Cut, fit and align paving materials
  • Clean the work area and check the quality of the finished surface

Requirements

  • Paving experience is welcome
  • Ability to work with levels and basic construction tools
  • Communicative English or Dutch language skills
  • Accuracy, independence and a practical work attitude

We offer

  • Dutch employment contract
  • Accommodation arranged by the employer
  • Health insurance
  • Support from Polish-speaking consultants
